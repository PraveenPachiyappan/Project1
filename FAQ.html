General Questions

What is Kotak 811?

Glad you asked! Kotak 811 is a zero balance digital account which offers interest rate of up to 4%* p.a .. Download the Kotak Mobile Banking App or visit Kotak Website to open your Kotak 811 account.

Register using your Aadhaar number*, PAN and other basic details. It will only take a few minutes! After setting up your mobile banking PIN, you can start using your account immediately.

* Submission of Aadhaar is voluntary

What are the features of Kotak 811?

811 is a fully loaded Digital Savings Account brought to you by Kotak Mahindra Bank. Below are the key features of your 811 account:

  • Open account instantly online
  • Earn up to 4%* interest p.a. on your savings account balance
  • No Minimum Balance required. Enjoy your Kotak 811 Zero Balance account
  • Kotak 811 Account holders can avail free funds transfer online using NEFT or IMPS or RTGS.
  • Request for your physical debit card through Net-banking or Mobile Banking App and get it for Rs 299/year (inclusive of taxes). Ensure you have adequate balance to pay for the Physical Debit Card before you request one.
  • Avail Scan and Pay** for your paymentsSeamless handling of your Kotak 811 account through Kotak Mobile Banking App

Am I eligible to apply for Kotak 811?

If you are 18 years of age or older, and are a resident of India with an Indian address, you are eligible to apply for a Kotak 811 account.

Make sure you have the following details handy when applying for Kotak 811 online:

  • PAN Number
  • Aadhaar Number*

Kotak 811 is available for individuals who do not have any existing relationship with Kotak Mahindra Bank.

Can I hold my account jointly with another applicant?

When you open a Kotak 811 account online, it is intended for a single user only. After the full KYC of your 811 account, you can add a joint account holder by visiting the nearest bank branch.

Can I save my application and continue it later?

Yes, of course!

All the information you’ve entered is regularly backed up. You may leave the app or close it at any point. When you return, you can resume from where you have left off.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely! The security of your personal information and your online banking transactions is of utmost importance to us. We maintain strict security standards to prevent unauthorized access to your information.

Debit Card & Cheque Book related

What is a Virtual Debit Card? How do I use it?

  • Kotak 811 Limited and full KYC account comes with a free Virtual Debit Card. Since it is not a physical card, it is called as Virtual Debit Card. It can be used for all online shopping and payments similar to how you use a physical debit card. It is completely safe & secure.

    • Follow the steps below to view Virtual Debit Card in the Mobile Banking App:

  • Log in to the Kotak Mobile Banking App using your CRN and MPIN
  • Once you’ve logged in, click on the ‘811’ tab
  • Navigate to ‘Virtual Debit card’ tab to see the details
  • Virtual Debit Cards cannot be used at ATMs or Point Of Sale (POS) machines. If you wish, you can apply for a physical debit card as well.

Can I have a physical Debit Card? How do I apply for one?

Yes, you can! Steps to request for a Physical Debit Card are as follows:

  • Login into the Kotak Mobile Banking App
  • Go to ‘Service Request’ section
  • Click on ’Virtual Debit Card’
  • Click on ‘GET ONE NOW’.

When will I receive a cheque book* for my 811 account?

Cheque Book is available to Full KYC accounts.

To avail Personalized Cheque Book, follow the steps below

  • Log in to the Kotak Mobile Banking App
  • Go to ‘811’ section
  • Tap on ‘REQUEST FOR A CHEQUE BOOK’

Generally, funds are credited instantaneously. In some cases, it may take up to 2 working days to credit money to your account. If funds are not reflected after two days, please get in touch with us with the transaction reference number.

Charges related

I did not maintain an average minimum balance. Will I be charged?

Kotak 811 Lite, Kotak 811 Limited KYC account and Kotak 811 Full KYC account are zero balance accounts. Hence, there is no need to keep a minimum balance.

However, Kotak 811 Edge Account requires you to maintain minimum Average Monthly Balance of Rs.10,000. Please Click here to know more about the charges on non-maintenance of the Average Monthly Balance.

What are the transaction charges in Kotak 811?

Most of the services provided to Kotak 811 customers are free such as online fund transfers, account opening, full KYC etc. However, some of the transactions carry a charge as per the Genral Schedule of Features & Charges (GSFC).Click here to know more.

Will I be charged for fund transfer?

Online fund transfer using RTGS, NEFT, & IMPS, bill/ merchant payments using UPI^ are free.

Fund transfer using UPI or through branches are charged as per GSFC.Click here to view the GSFC.

Will I be charged for debit card or ATM transactions?

Virtual Debit Card is free of cost. The charge of the physical debit card will depend on the type of the Debit Card. To know more about transaction charges, refer to the General Schedule of Features & Charges (GSFC).

My ECS has bounced. Will I be charged?

that’s unfortunate. You will be charged as per the current General Schedule of Features & Charges (GSFC). Refer to the ‘ECS/cheque issued and return (due to non-availability of funds)’ category in GSFC.Click here to view the GSFC.

Will I be charged for bank statements and alerts?

Email account statements are free of cost. Physical statement and SMS alerts are charged as per General Schedule of Features & Charges.Click here to view GSFC.
